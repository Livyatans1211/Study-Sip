About Study & Sip Coffee House

Study & Sip Coffee House is a new chain of independent coffee shops popping up across major cities in the UK. We specialise creating a cosy and welcoming space where customers can enjoy great food and drink made with local ingredients straight from the heart of the community.

Are you a student? Sip & Study Coffee House has a free ‘book a table’ service, perfect for studying or working on last minute essays with our free complimentary Wi-Fi and convenient charging ports. Feeling thirsty? Our café lets you order a drink or a sweet treat straight to your table with our speedy online ordering service. We also offer student discounts to anyone with a valid UNiDAYS or Student Beans account.

We also have larger comfortable seating options for big groups of people looking to catch up with friends or colleagues, both indoors and outdoors.

Why choose Study & Sip Coffee House?

  • Ethically sourced ingredients from trusted local suppliers
  • Our baristas are trained to make every coffee fresh to a high standard
  • We offer a wide range of food and drink options to suit all tastes
  • We are committed to keeping prices affordable and offer regular discounts

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free Wi-Fi for studying or working?

Yes, all Study & Sip Coffee House locations provide free Wi-Fi and charging ports, making our cafés a comfortable place to study or work.

Do you offer milk alternatives?

We offer oat, almond and soy milk so customers can customise their coffee to suit their dietary preferences. We also use separate equipment to prepare our milk alternatives to avoid cross-contamination with dairy products.

Do you have seating for groups or meetings?

Our cafés include both indoor and outdoor seating suitable for individuals, study groups and casual meetings.

Do you allow pets inside?

We welcome well-behaved pets in our outdoor seating areas, but we do not allow pets inside our cafés apart from registered service animals. We also provide water bowls outside for our furry friends!
